# Day 5 — Conditional Probability

## Topics Covered
- Conditional Probability

## Key Learnings
- Probability of an event given another event has already occurred.

## Formula
P(A | B) = P(A ∩ B) / P(B)

## Example
If we know a student passed Math, what's the probability they also passed Physics?

## Importance in ML
- Used in Bayesian models
- Important in classification problems
- Core concept in real-world decision making

## Insight
Information changes probability.

## Reflection
Conditional thinking is critical — decisions should depend on available information.
