# Day 6 — Bayes’ Theorem

## What I Learned
Bayes’ Theorem helps update probability when new evidence is available.

Formula:
P(A|B) = (P(B|A) * P(A)) / P(B)

## Key Insight
Even accurate tests can mislead when the event is rare.

## Example
Disease testing showed that a positive result does not always mean high probability of disease due to false positives.

## Applications
- Spam filtering
- Fraud detection
- Machine Learning (Naive Bayes)

## Conclusion
Bayes’ Theorem is essential for reasoning under uncertainty.
