# Day 3 — Covariance & Correlation

## Topics Covered
- Covariance
- Correlation

## Key Learnings

### Covariance
- Measures how two variables change together.
- Positive → move in same direction
- Negative → move in opposite direction

### Correlation
- Standardized version of covariance
- Range: -1 to +1

| Value | Meaning |
|------|--------|
| +1 | Perfect positive relationship |
| 0  | No relationship |
| -1 | Perfect negative relationship |

## Difference
- Covariance: magnitude unclear
- Correlation: normalized and interpretable

## Importance in ML
- Feature selection
- Detecting relationships
- Reducing redundancy

## Insight
Correlation is more useful in practice than covariance.

## Reflection
Understanding relationships between variables is key to building good models.
